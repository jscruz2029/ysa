10 Things I Hate About You

10 Things I Hate About You is a witty 90s romcom based on Shakespeare’s The Taming of the Shrew. Heath Ledger stars as Patrick, a rebellious teen hired to date Julia Stiles' Kat, an independent and tough high schooler. What starts as a scheme soon turns into genuine romance, filled with humor, chemistry, and classic teen drama.

Flipped

A heartwarming coming-of-age story, Flipped follows the evolving relationship between two childhood neighbors, Bryce and Juli. Set in the 1960s, the film shows how their feelings for each other shift over the years, from childhood annoyance to a something deeper.

The Notebook

The Notebook tells the epic love story of Noah and Allie, two young lovers from different social classes whose romance is tested by time and circumstance. Told through flashbacks, the film shows their enduring connection as an elderly Noah reads their story to Allie in a nursing home.
